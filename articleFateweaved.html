A card game inspired by Tarot’s Major and Minor Arcanas


Fateweaved is a roguelike card game in which players manage two tarot decks simultaneously. Discover the circumstances of the investigation you are facing, build connections with the cards, and progress through encounters with increasingly powerful combinations.


We’re building it from the ground up. I handle all the programming, using Unity to bring the game mechanics to life.

My teammate Anaïs Arnoux and I co-design the game together, while she take care of the art and visuals. We’ll be sharing more details soon, but for now, here are a few screenshots to give you a taste of what’s coming:
