A 40min. experience based on Ubisoft's show


Captain Laserhawk: A Blood Dragon Remix is an animated television series inspired by the 2013 video game Far Cry 3:Blood Dragon, while amalgamating elements and characters from several other Ubisoft franchises.

Based on this, we created a Sandbox Experience which is taking place during the first episode. You’re a down on your luck investigator when a woman stumbles into your office with a mysterious floppy disk. It’s the break you’ve been looking for as your search for missing hackers with underground icons Jade and Pey’J has hit a brick wall.

But you may have found more than you bargained for. Suddenly, you’re caught up in a city-wide mystery, holding the clue that everyone wants to get their hands on. Can you solve the puzzles, unmask the villains, and save the world from evil megacorp Eden – or other, even more sinister powers?

There’s only one way to find out. Put on your detective shoes, fire up your hacker synapses, and go toe-to-toe with the unseen forces that lurk behind those in power.

Bringing the show to life



For this project, staying true to the show’s universe was a top priority. Story, visuals, and tone all had to feel like a seamless extension of the original world.
The game takes place before Episode 2, showing what led to Jade and Pey’j’s capture without affecting the main arc.

Building a cyberpunk atmosphere in tight spaces was a challenge. Every alley, light, and NPC had to contribute to the city’s oppressive feel. Eden’s presence is suggested through environmental cues, while gameplay mechanics like infiltration and hacking were tailored to characters and locations.

We also added new missions, villains, dialogues, and locations that expand the universe naturally, giving players fresh experiences without straying from the original story.
